Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of the site is to create a space where rafting lovers may share their experiences, questions about the sport and also find people with whom they share their passion. The customers will be who enhance the site sharing their videos, photos, and testimonies.

Audience

Men and women, College students, between 20 and 35 years old. They go out with friends every weekend searching for new adventures. They put a lot of attention on their social media profile, so they try to share high-quality content when they post about their sport experiences. They are digital customers, they buy online, they pay with digital wallets, they read on their phones, and search for new events on social medias. They are people with a huge sense of adventure, they are willing to try new things and especially to share them with other people.

Typography

Heading Font: Oswald

Oswald font is a narrow but bolded font, giving a sense of strongness and impact.

Paragraph Font: Roboto

The Roboto font is more light, but as is the same used on Instagram, it is more related to youth and freshness.
